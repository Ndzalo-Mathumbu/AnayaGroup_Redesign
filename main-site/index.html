About Us

Rooted in the Soil, Growing a Nation.

Anaya Group is a proudly Congolese conglomerate transforming land, food, and infrastructure into sustainable engines of growth. Through its three key major divisions — Anaya Agriculture, Anaya Estates, and Anaya Build — we deliver solutions that empower communities. Our work promotes food security, secure land ownership, and quality-built environments. Combining local expertise with ethical practices, every project benefits the people we serve. From agriculture, real estate, to construction, our initiatives create lasting impact and economic opportunities. At Anaya Group, we are committed to excellence and sustainable growth.
